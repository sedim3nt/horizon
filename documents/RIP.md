# Project Idea

### Project Submitted By

Suede and Sean

## Summary

Build Horizon, a public RaidGuild BD signal feed that turns Queen Raida's
Discord, meeting, and CRM records into chronological opportunity threads with
current stages, owners, next actions, timelines, and automated RaidGuild-style
imagery.

## Why should we build this?

RaidGuild's BD context is fragmented across channels, meetings, transcripts,
and CRM records. Horizon will make active opportunities understandable without
requiring members or prospective clients to reconstruct that context manually.

The project reuses the ClawRyderz pipeline, Quartz, and RaidGuild's design system
to keep the build focused. Suede will lead product, engineering, design quality,
automation, and deployment. Sean will lead BD editorial standards, review the
generated feed, and ensure the result is accurate, useful, and persuasive to
clients.
